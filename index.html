Backstory

Design is more than knowing that green text and a red background is a terrible idea. It's concepts, sketching, planning, hundreds of revisions and multiple coffee breaks to fuel the madness that is flowing out of your mind and onto the screen.

A kinda long time ago, in the not-so-distant northwest that is Washington state, I started using Photoshop to ink illustrations. Fast forward to now, and I have extensive experience with not only Photoshop, but with Adobe CC, AfterEffects, Premiere Pro, HTML5, CSS3, Jquery, and I make a mean kitchen sink too.
I am a big fan of motion graphics, as well as UX/UI design. It's still a shiny new field in design that is being filled, and most companies that have dipped their toes into the proverbial soup that is this new school of thought have come out changed for the better. I am versed in the language of interaction design and have experience designing practical examples for a multinational company.
